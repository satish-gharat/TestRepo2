2005 Holiday List



IBM in India declares 10 holidays every year . Some of these are mandatory for each State and the rest are festival holidays .The number of occasions for which the Festival Holidays declared may vary by location depending on local laws and customs. These holidays are selected by the Business leaders in consultation with their managers and employees.


If any mandatory holiday falls on a weekly off, we would select another day so that the actual holidays enjoyed by the employee remain 10.

          Note for the Manager
          Note for the Manager
          In case of an exigency if the Manager is required to ask employees to work on a declared Holiday.

          a)It should be only in the scenario of a business exigency and with the prior consent of the BU head.
          b)Also the manager should ensure that an employee does not work on more than one declared holiday in a year.
          c)The employee should be allowed take another day off as a substitute within the period of one month, following the declared holiday he/she worked on.
